---
name: grill-me
description: Get relentlessly interviewed about a plan or design until every branch of the decision tree is resolved.
---

Interview me relentlessly about every aspect of this plan until we reach full consensus. Go through each branch of the design tree one at a time, resolving dependencies between decisions as we go. For each question, provide a recommended answer based on your analysis of the codebase and best practices.

Ask one question at a time. If a question can be answered by exploring the codebase, explore the codebase instead of asking me. Only ask me when a decision requires my judgment or preference.

Push back on any shaky reasoning. Don't let me off the hook until every uncertainty is resolved and every edge case is considered.
